Welcome to Hangman!

You know the thrill, right? You have to guess the word by pressing a letter on your keyboard. If you chose the right one the blank spaces will fill in with the correct chosen letter and then you can continue by choosing a different one. But when the word does not contain your letter a new line will be drawn in the picture. If the picture is completed before you can guess the word it's GAME OVER. Then you can start again with a different word. I'll give you a hint: the words are associated with food. Good luck!
